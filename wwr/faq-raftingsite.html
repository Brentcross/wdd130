Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for rafting?

The minimum age for rafting is 8 years old. Participants under 18 years old must have a parent or legal guardian sign a waiver.

What should I wear for rafting?

You should wear quick-drying clothes, such as synthetic or polyester. Avoid wearing cotton, as it retains moisture and can make you feel cold. We also recommend wearing closed-toe shoes with good traction, such as water shoes or sneakers.

Do I need experience to go rafting?

No, you do not need experience to go rafting. Our guides will provide a safety briefing and instructions on paddling techniques before you get on the water. We offer trips for all skill levels, from beginner to advanced.

What is included in the price of a rafting trip?

The price of a rafting trip includes a professional guide, all necessary equipment (raft, paddles, life jackets, helmets), transportation to and from the river, and a riverside picnic lunch.
